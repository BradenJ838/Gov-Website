My history

Braden Johnston was born and raised in a small town in the heart of America's heartland. He grew up in a working-class family, where he learned the value of hard work, perseverance, and self-reliance from an early age. His parents instilled in him a deep sense of patriotism and a belief in the American dream.

After graduating high school he attended the prestigious University of American Samoa Law School. Armed with his education and a strong work ethic, he embarked on a successful career as a law maker.

As a congressman, Braden has been a tireless advocate for limited government, free-market principles, and individual liberty. He has championed policies to cut taxes, reduce regulations, and promote economic growth, earning him praise from his colleagues and constituents alike.

Now seeking the presidency, Congressman Braden Johnston is a proven leader with a clear vision for America's future. With his strong business background, conservative values, and commitment to putting America first, he is ready to lead the country forward and tackle the challenges of the 21st century head-on.
