High gas rates are hurting the economy
Democrat's tax policies hurt the poor

Together we can reduce gas prices by 3 dollars

The bulk of the cost of gas is due to high taxes put in by the democrats. In California 2-3$ per gallon is due to taxes.

High gas prices hurt Americans regardless of income level, but they also disproportionately hurt the lowest income households because the rise in gas prices in turn affects the prices of things such as food, clothing, and other necessities.

By giving tax cuts to crucial industries such as agriculture, domestic production and oil, we can revitalize the economy,lower prices of important goods, and save the poor.
