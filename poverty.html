Poverty

Target the root cause of poverty and inequality
Poverty is caused by poor economic management and our education system

Promoting economic growth is the most effective way to alleviate poverty

We need to stimulate job creation, entrepreneurship, and investment by lowering taxes, reducing regulations, and fostering a business-friendly environment..

Education reform as a means of breaking the cycle of poverty. We support school choice initiatives, such as voucher programs and charter schools, to provide low-income families with access to better educational opportunities.

We will implement Enterprise Zones that are areas designated for economic revitalization, through tax incentives and regulatory relief, using a private solution that will not increase government spending significantly
