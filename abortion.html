Abortion is Racism
The cycle of poverty is exacerbated by Abortion

Democrats are killing minority families

Abortions are disproportionally common in minority and low income families. This can result in a reduction in the population of certain minority groups over time, impacting cultural diversity and representation within society. High abortion rates among minorities can exacerbate existing social and economic disparities within these communities. It can also lead to a decrease in the number of future workers and taxpayers, potentially hindering economic growth and perpetuating cycles of poverty.

Democrats implement ineffective regulations such as Targeted Regulation of Abortion Providers (TRAP) laws which disproportionately affect clinics serving minorities, limiting access to safe abortion services. We need to implement effective, safe regulations to protect the sanctitiy of life and protect minority families.

We need to put in effethe root causes of unintended pregnancies within minority communities, such as economic disadvantage, lack of access to education and healthcare, and breakdowns in family structure by creating policies that promote economic empowerment, educational opportunities, and access to healthcare services to support families in making life-affirming choices.
